// Research Focus

Neuromorphic Sensing & VSA

↑ scroll to blend · mouse to orbit
Event Cameras
Asynchronous silicon retinas that output per-pixel brightness changes at microsecond resolution — eliminating motion blur and enabling 10 kHz+ sensing.
Spiking Networks
Leaky-integrate-and-fire neurons process sparse event streams with 10–100× lower power than conventional CNNs, enabling always-on edge inference.
Vector Symbolic AI
High-dimensional hypervectors encode structured world knowledge via binding ⊗ and superposition ⊕ — interpretable, noise-robust, one-shot learning.

// NeuroSymbolic Pipeline

Processing Architecture

Stage 1 of 3
Event-Camera Front-End
Latency: <0.1 ms · Power: 5 mW
Event Camera Front-End — The DVS retina fires asynchronous (t,x,y,p) events at each pixel when Δlog(I) > θ. No frames, no motion blur. Raw event rate ~1–10 M ev/s is filtered with a 1 ms refractory window and encoded as a 2-channel surface-of-active-events (SAE) tensor feeding directly into the SNN.

// Lab Hardware

Neuromorphic Platform Stack

Prophesee EVK4
1 Mpx event camera · 100 dB DR · USB3 · 10 kHz+ event rate. Primary sensing platform for urban navigation experiments.
Intel Loihi-2
1M neurons · on-chip learning · <1 mW active. Used for gesture classification and SNN benchmark evaluation.
SpiNNaker-2
152-core ARM cortex-M4 array. Hosts large-scale VSA binding experiments and multi-agent spike routing.
DAVIS 346
346×260 DVS + APS frames · IMU · ROS2 driver. Stereo rig for depth-from-events and ego-motion estimation.
Jetson Orin NX
16-TOPS edge inference · 10–15 W TDP. Runs PyTorch SNN and VSA encoding at 60 Hz on the UAV payload.
DJI F550 Hex
Custom neuromorphic payload: EVK4 + Orin NX + Loihi-2 daughterboard. Main aerial testbed for perception pipeline.

01 — Research Areas

What I focus on

01
Event-driven Systems & Perception

Combining the feature extraction benefits of CNNs with the low latency processing of SCNNs. Utilizing Neuromorphic Vision Sensors for asynchronous processing and semantic segmentation of dynamic scenes.

02
Spiking Neural Networks

Developing SNN models and low-power, robust learning frameworks to tackle challenges across machine learning, computer vision, robotics, and signal processing.

03
Single Photon 3D Imaging

Driving high-speed LiDAR-based imaging modalities using the time-of-arrival of individual photons reconstructed asynchronously by SCNNs for robust 3D tracking.

04
AI at the Network Edge

Creating deployable intelligence that is powerful, efficient, and adaptive for out-in-the-wild deployment applications spanning from defence and aerospace to everyday robotics.

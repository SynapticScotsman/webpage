Boosting Embodied Resilience in Extreme Environments

Translating chaotic event streams into resilient robotic spatial awareness. My research bridges biologically-inspired neuromorphic perception with discrete symbolic reasoning, enabling autonomous systems that sense less to understand more.

The Neuro-Symbolic Pipeline

Aim 1 → Aim 2 → Aim 3

Continuous Perception

Asynchronous event streams filtered through log-polar receptive fields to isolate stable geometric structures under rapid ego-motion.

Symbolic Reasoning

Neural spikes translated into high-dimensional Spatial Semantic Pointers (SSPs), mapping complex what-to-where relationships via phase encoding.

Active Decision Loop

When mapping uncertainty spikes, the system triggers active investigation — altering speed or activating SPAD / LiDAR fallbacks.

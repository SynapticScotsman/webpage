01 — RESEARCH AREAS

What I Focus On

01 // EVENT-DRIVEN

Event-driven Systems & Perception

Combining CNN feature extraction with SCNN low-latency processing. Utilizing neuromorphic vision sensors for asynchronous semantic segmentation of dynamic scenes.

02 // NETWORKS

Spiking Neural Networks

Developing SNN models and low-power, robust learning frameworks for machine learning, computer vision, robotics, and signal processing.

03 // IMAGING

Single Photon 3D Imaging

High-speed LiDAR-based imaging using time-of-arrival of individual photons, reconstructed asynchronously by SCNNs for robust 3D tracking.

AI at the Network Edge

Deployable intelligence that is powerful, efficient, and adaptive — spanning defence and aerospace to everyday robotics.

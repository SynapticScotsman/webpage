Integrated Framework

The Neuro-Symbolic Handshake

Continuous Perception

Asynchronous event streams filtered through log-polar receptive fields to isolate stable geometric structures under rapid ego-motion.

Symbolic Reasoning

Neural spikes translated into high-dimensional Spatial Semantic Pointers (SSPs), mapping complex what-to-where relationships.

Active Decision Loop

When mapping uncertainty spikes, the system triggers active investigation — altering speed or activating SPAD / LiDAR fallbacks.

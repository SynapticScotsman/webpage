01 — Research Areas

What this lab works on

01
Computational Neuromorphic Imaging

Developing the mathematical and experimental foundations of a new subfield that couples event-based sensing with computational imaging principles — forward models, inverse problems, and scene reconstruction.

02
Event-Based Wavefront & Spectral Sensing

Novel optical architectures that use dynamic event streams instead of frame-based readout for wavefront sensing and hyperspectral imaging. Targeting scenarios where speed and power matter.

03
Closed-Loop Active Sensing

Spiking neural networks driving real-time feedback loops for tracking, line-of-sight stabilisation, and adaptive image reconstruction. Hardware-in-the-loop across FPGA and neuromorphic processors.

04
Physically Informed Neural Networks

Integrating physics-based forward models (OTF, angular spectrum, Fresnel) directly into neural architectures — bridging classical optics with data-driven inversion in dynamic scenes.
